Gerry Burr

Controls Engineer

Gerry Burr is a Controls Engineer at Acieta located in Council Bluffs, IA. There he designs Automation systems and also programs the computer that runs the system(PLC). He has been an Engineer for over a decade. Gerry Responsible Controls Engineer well-versed in the design, development, installation, management and maintenance of devices and equipment used in the monitoring and control of engineering machinery, systems and processes. Can delivery new systems, test and modify existing systems, and work with engineers in design and operations, purchasers and other staff. Gerry graduated with a Manufacturing Engineering degree from University of Wisconsin-Stout. When Gerry is not working, he is busy working on his project car. He re-built his racing car on his own. When his car is running, he takes out on the track to test its time on the drag strip. Gerry enjoys anything that involves him solving a problem. In addition to staying busy with Engineering, Gerry enjoys riding his BMX bike at the skate park. He strives to land harder tricks with his bike.
